Get in the Coop!

AR Mobile Game

Game Designer | Lead Developer

Developed with Unity

Showcased at ENTER PLAYMODE 2022

Move around the fence and try to get your chickens back into the coop!

You will set the play area at the beginning. You also need to manually open and close the gate so watch out for the problematic ones that might escape! At the top of the screen you will see your number of chickens herded and a timer above it tracking your speed. Do your best!
