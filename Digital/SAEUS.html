SAEUS

Windows/Mac OS

Jing Fan: Concept, Game Design, Code, Unity

Panyapha Leekitwattana: 3D Asset Creation, Concept Art

Daniel Han: Environment Building, Skill Icons

[Currently Require 3 Displays to Play]

[Currently Require 3 Joysticks to Play]

SAEUS is a 3D asymmetrical multiplayer game about people trying to prove the existence of a deer-like, mythical creature called ‘Saeus’ by taking photos, while the creature is trying to avoid them.

    Characters

  1. Saeus

    A mythical creature that feeds on blue glowing mushrooms.

    Ability

    • Can rush out and destroy pictures when hit other players
    • Can become invisible but only for a certain time

    Goal

    Survive (none of the two people have taken 5 photos of you) until sunrise

  2. Ranger

    A guy who is good at running and is obsessed with mythical creatures.

    Ability

    • Can set traps that will trap others who step on it
    • Run faster

    Goal

    Be the first one who takes 5 photos of Saeus

  3. YouTuber

    A guy who wants to attract fans by showing photos of mythical creatures.

    Ability

    • Can steal photos from the ranger from a distance
    • Can put down cameras that can detect if something is close to it
    • Take photos
    • Run slower

    Goal

    Be the first one who takes 5 photos of Saeus
