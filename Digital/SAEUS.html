SAEUS

Local Multi-Player Multi-Screen Asymmetrical Game

Game Designer | Lead Developer

Developed with Unity

[Require 3 Displays and 3 Joysticks to Play]

SAEUS is a 3D asymmetrical multiplayer game about people trying to prove the existence of a deer-like, mythical creature called ‘Saeus’ by taking photos, while the creature is trying to avoid them. All three characters have different abilities. In order to win the game, the two human characters need to compete for the first one taking five photos of the creature, whereas the creature wins the game by surviving till daytime.
