Prison Escape

Windows/Mac OS

Jing Fan: Character Animation, Level Design (Prototype), Background Music, Code

Analisse Guadeloupe: Background Asset, Title & Lose Scene, Level Design (Final), Code

Prison Escape is a 2D platformer about hiding from enemies. Player will play as a criminal trying to get out of jail. Play through three levels to escape and enjoy the fine blue sky.

    Controls:
  • left & right arrow keys/A & D for moving
  • Space for jumping
  • Shift for crouching when you are behind a crate
