Inkression

Narrative Adventure Game

Game Designer | Lead Developer | Producer

Developed with Unity

Selected by NYU Incubator 2024, MAGFEST 2025; Showcased at PlayNYC 2024, GDoC Expo 2024

Enter the world of Milly, a troubled tattoo artist who sets out to revive her dying neighborhood by collecting remaining stories through her art. Walk the streets of Nortown, witness the comes and goes of its people, and redefine the meaning behind each tattoo in this first person narrative game.

Nortown, a once vibrant community, now faces imminent demolition to make way for a bustling commercial hub. Its residents must now find new directions for their lives amidst this upheaval.

As Milly Sommerville, a detached tattoo artist who sets out to complete a forgotten wish from years ago, you will explore Nortown and learn the past and present of its people through the remnants of the neighborhood.

COLOR REVELATION: Witness your surrounding's true colors as you receive newfound impressions.

STORIES OF LIFE TOLD THROUGH TATTOOS: Record stories through new tattoo designs based on your impressions of the neighborhood.

TATTOO: Practice your skills as a tattoo artist at The Blindfolded, give your clients their desired ink as you learn more about them through chit-chats.

HERE, THEM, YOU: The intersection of different lives, struggles, and decisions intertwining to tell the story of an everyday neighborhood.
