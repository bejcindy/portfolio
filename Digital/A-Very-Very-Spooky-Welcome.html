A Very Very Spooky Welcome

Oculus Rift

Jing Fan: Character Modeling, VR Coding

Michelle Tu: Concept Art, Environment Building

Shantanu Nair: Asset Creation

David Zhan: Manager, Unity

Some new guy (I think he's named Mark?) just moved into my house! Well, yeah he bought the place, so what? I've already lived on this piece of land for hundreds of years so who the heck does he think he is for just waltzing in here?

Play as a ghost and spooky the wits out of Mark by using your ghostly powers.

    OVR Controls:
  • Left stick to move around
  • Grab left/right controller to grab objects, release to throw
  • Pull left/right trigger to use "the force" and move objects from a distance
