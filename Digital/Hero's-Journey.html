Hero's Journey

Multi-Ending Meta Game

Game Mechanic Design, Programmer, Asset Designer

Developed with Unity and Aseprite

Go backward in time to figure out what happened to the king

This is an adventure game with three normal endings and four hidden endings. The game is about the hero trying to figure out what happened before he lost his memory through a magical scroll that allows him to go back in time. He will walk backward and talk to people he had talked to before to figure out what is happening in the kingdom.
