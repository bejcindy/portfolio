I'm a game designer, Unity developer, and UI designer with a passion for creating unique interactive experiences. Co-founder of BROKENCIGS, currently working on Inkression. I hold an MFA in Game Design from NYU Game Center and a BFA in Design and Technology with a focus on Game Design, along with a minor in Data Visualization from Parsons School of Design, The New School.

With six years of experience in exploring and creating a variety of games, I am passionate about capturing and expressing essences of daily life through games with a humorous and metaphorical approach. My background of UI design and data visualization enables me to create intuitive and engaging user experiences in games.
