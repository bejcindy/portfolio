Maze

Asymmetrical Board Game

Personal Project

This game is created for 3 to 5 players. The players are separated into two groups: the boss and the brave. At the beginning of the game, the boss draws 3 map cards and all other players draw 3 action cards. Each turn the boss need to put down a map card that is either distracting players from going to the destination or creating challenges that requires the brave to play an action card. The brave need to go to the destination map card to win the game. Each player in the brave group have 3 life points and losing all of them means the player is eliminated from the game. Each turn the brave can choose one person to flip a map card from the deck of cards and put it down next to any map card played before.
