Trash

Card Game

Personal Project

An educational game about garbage classification

This is a card game I designed to educate people, especially children, of knowledge of Garbage Classification.

    Rules of the game:
  1. Each person draw 3~5 cards (depends on the number of people playing the game)
  2. Flip a card from the deck and put it down on the desk as the first card
  3. People then put down cards that have the trash that belongs to the same category as the previous card
  4. If someone plays the wrong card and caught by another player, then that person needs to take all trash with him/her
  5. Whoever empties his/her hand first wins the game
