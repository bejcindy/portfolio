Run!

Board Game

Personal Project

Use walls to trap others while trying to survive!

In this game, each player is controlling a figure represented by different colors. The goal is to eliminate other players and survive to the end of the game. The players start off from the middle squares at each side. Each turn the player can roll the dice deciding the maximum step he/she can move. After he/she stops moving, he/she can put down a wall blocking other players from moving through it. If one player is completely trapped by walls, then he/she is eliminated from the game. The person that put down the last wall trapping the eliminated player can then get one chance to remove one wall in the whole game.
